Welcome to Our Workshop

Enabling my daughter's Cosplay through access to better tools

I don't Cosplay, but my daughter does and I let her use my workshop to work on her projects. I started with my father's tools, but over the decades I added more and more tools to help me with my woodworking and electronics projects. I now share my workshop with my daughter, and the posts here highlight the tools and equipment I provide her to use with her projects.
